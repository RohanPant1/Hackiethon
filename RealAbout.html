About ZoneIn

Compete. Motivate. Deliver.

What exactly is ZoneIn?

Our website gives students and workers all over the world a chance to keep motivated through different struggles from working at an unfamiliar location or even without having enough resources for help. ZoneIn collaborates the work of users all throughout the world, by sharing your work you can be the key to motivating an individual down on motivation or without a real idea to a solution.

ZoneIn works with people of all ages bringing together works of different generations while making it a safe and user-friendly destination on the net.

ZoneIn also includes various features that allow you to have a more customizable and unique experience. A main feature of the application includes being able to compare your work with other users while giving comments as well as supporting them by liking or sharing their zones (their post).

However, other features such as the goals function allow you to set goals for yourself over a certain period of time while being able to monitor your progress as well as receiving reminders for when you have achieved them. More collaborative features include the groups function where you can team up with friends, classmates, colleagues or even finding new mates to work with together in the future.

All these functions ensure that you can have a surprisingly fun and more productive time working on assignments or projects while continuing to stay motivated and on top of your work.
